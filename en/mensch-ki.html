Human in the Loop

The agent prepares.
Sie entscheiden.

AIMOS agents do not replace employees — they are digitale Assistenten that take over routine work so your team can focus on what truly matters.

Workflow

How the Collaboration Works

Every agent action follows the same workflow: prepare, present, approve.

1

The Agent Researches

The agent gathers data, checks inventory, searches emails, calculates variants. It works with the tools that have been assigned to it.

2

The Agent Presents

The result is presented as a draft: a booking proposal, an email draft, a requirements report, an inspection report. Nothing is executed automatically.

3

The Employee Decides

You review, correct, supplement — and approve. Or reject. The agent learns from your corrections and improves next time.

No automatic sending. No automatic booking. No automatic ordering.
The human always has the last word.

Daily Work

What Changes — and What Stays

Your team stays. Your expertise stays. What changes: the routine work.

Before (without AIMOS)

  • 45 minutes daily: manually querying and summarizing account balances
  • Every customer inquiry: first search, then type, then review
  • Knowledge is trapped in the heads of individual employees
  • Routine tasks block the actual specialized work

After (with AIMOS)

  • Agent delivers the summary — you review and supplement
  • Agent prepares the response — you adjust tone and content
  • Agent stores facts permanently — your knowledge is never lost
  • The agent handles routine work — you focus on your expertise

Uniquely Human

What Only a Human Can Do

An agent is a tool — not a colleague. These abilities remain uniquely human.

Customer Relationships

Building trust, reading between the lines, striking the right tone. No algorithm replaces a personal conversation.

Negotiation

Prioritizing, finding compromises, weighing interests. The agent delivers the numbers — you lead the negotiation.

Judgment

Decisions under uncertainty, moral considerations, good judgment. The agent knows the rules — you know the exceptions.

Improvisation

When something unexpected happens, it takes creativity and experience — not a model that can only repeat what it already knows.

Domain Expertise

Your knowledge built over years — industry knowledge, supplier relationships, process understanding — no AI can replace that. On the contrary: the agent improves the more it benefits from your experience.

Responsibility

In the end, a human bears the responsibility. For the booking, the order, the email. The agent supports — the responsibility stays with you.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Will AIMOS replace my job?

No. AIMOS agents take over routine tasks — gathering data, creating standard responses, monitoring deadlines. The specialized work, the decisions, and the customer relationships remain with you. In practice, employees report having more time for the tasks they were actually hired for.

Who decides — me or the AI?

Always you. The agent can make suggestions, create email drafts, or prepare booking templates — but none of this is executed without your approval. This is not an option, but a fundamental principle of the architecture.

Can the AI make mistakes?

Yes. That is exactly why human-in-the-loop exists: every agent output is a draft that is reviewed by a human. The agent is a tool — like a calculator. You would check the result there too.

Do I need technical skills?

No. You communicate via Telegram, email, or voice — in normal language. No terminal, no programming, no training required.

What happens to my knowledge when I leave the company?

Your expertise that you share with the agent in daily work is stored in long-term memory. This protects the company from knowledge loss — but it does not make you replaceable. Because the memory stores facts, not experience, not judgment, not relationships.
