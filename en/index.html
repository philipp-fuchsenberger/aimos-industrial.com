No Lock-In

Open Source. Your Server.
Your Decision.

AIMOS belongs to you — today, tomorrow, and even if you change your mind.

MIT License

Full access to every line of code. No license fees — not today, not tomorrow, not with 100 agents. You can modify, extend, or fork the code.

No Vendor Lock-In

AIMOS does not tie you to any provider. All data is stored in open formats (JSON, SQLite, PostgreSQL). No proprietary formats, no hidden dependencies.

Agent Migration

Your agents are portable. The Open Agent Package (OAP) exports an agent completely — memory, skills, configuration. Import on another server in minutes. If you want to switch to a proprietary system tomorrow: your knowledge base goes with you.

Your Data, Your Server

All data stays on your SovereignNode. No cloud provider, no third party, no data transfer. If AIMOS no longer suits you, pull the plug — and everything stays with you.

The risk is not adopting AIMOS. The risk is waiting — because every day without AIMOS is a day your agents aren't building knowledge.
