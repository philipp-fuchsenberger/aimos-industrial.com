The Real Question

What does it cost to change nothing?

AIMOS is not a chatbot. It is an assistant system that takes routine work off your team — gathering data, preparing reports, monitoring deadlines — so your employees can focus on their actual specialized work.

The point is not to replace employees.

AIMOS takes over routine tasks that today keep qualified employees occupied below their potential: looking up balances, answering standard inquiries, summarizing documents, tracking appointments. Your employees gain time for what you hired them for — customer relationships, decisions, creative work.

The AIMOS agent has a permanent memory: it knows every customer, every preference, every open item. When an employee leaves, the documented knowledge is preserved — but human experience and relationships are not. That is why your team is indispensable.

What Do These Numbers Mean?

A token is the smallest processing unit of the language model — roughly one word or word part. A typical Telegram message comprises 30–100 tokens, a detailed agent response 100–300 tokens.

Per agent interaction, the system processes approx. 4,000–6,000 tokens (system prompt + memory + conversation history + response). With 100 interactions per day, that is approx. 500,000 tokens — the GPU is only at 10% utilization.

Even with intensive use with 10 agents, 65% GPU capacity remains free. A SovereignNode is more than adequately sized for the entire mid-market. The AI waits for your employees — not the other way around.

No ongoing compute costs: Unlike cloud AI, you don't pay per token. After the one-time hardware purchase (~5,000 EUR), only electricity costs of approx. 45 EUR/month apply — regardless of how many tokens your agents process.
