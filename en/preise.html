Why Different Model Sizes?

The 27B model (Starter & Business) is fast and efficient — ideal for clearly defined tasks such as customer support, ERP queries, and appointment management.

The 70B model (Professional & Enterprise) understands more complex contexts, follows instructions more precisely, and delivers more nuanced responses. It is particularly suited for demanding scenarios such as technical consulting, multilingual communication, and creative tasks.

All tiers use the same software. An upgrade from Starter to Business or Professional is possible at any time — by swapping hardware, without reconfiguring the agents.
